Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about selling cars, shopping listings, spare parts, price estimates, and damage detection.

How do I create a car listing?

Open Sell Your Car, fill in make/model/year/km/fuel/transmission, set your expected price, and publish. Your listing will appear in Shop.

Tip: Add a clear photo to get faster responses.
How does the price estimate work?

The estimate uses year, kilometers, fuel type, transmission and owners to calculate a market range. If an external price API is configured, we use that; otherwise we use a built-in heuristic.

You can still edit your final selling price before publishing.
Why can’t I add to cart without login?

Cart is linked to your account so your items remain saved. Please login to add cars or spare parts to your cart and view them in My Account.

How do I run Damage Detection?

Go to Damage Detection, upload one or more images, then click Run Detection. The results highlight detected areas with labels and confidence.

If the external damage API is not configured, demo detections may appear for testing.
How do I contact the seller?

In the Shop page, click Contact Seller on a listing to view contact details (when available).
