Warranty Information

Warranty terms depend on the product and seller. Below is a simple coverage guide for spare parts purchased through AutoPro Elite and recommended steps to raise a claim.

Coverage overview

Typical coverage periods (may vary by brand/OEM). Always keep the invoice and original packaging.
Category Typical Warranty Notes
Electrical (battery, sensors, bulbs) 3–12 months Warranty applies to manufacturing defects only.
Engine & Components 3–6 months Requires professional installation and proof.
Brakes (pads/discs) Limited / No warranty Wear-and-tear items are usually excluded.
Suspension 3–6 months May require inspection report for claim.

How to raise a warranty claim

1) Keep invoice
Invoice/order details + product SKU are required.
2) Share photos
Upload clear images of the part, packaging, and defect.
3) Installation proof
If applicable, provide a mechanic/service center invoice.
4) Verification
We verify brand warranty and confirm replacement/refund eligibility.

Common exclusions

Warranty is usually not applicable if any of these are found:
Wear & tear
Brake pads, wipers, clutch plates, etc.
Improper installation
Incorrect fitment or missing torque/spec procedure.
Accidental damage
Crash, water ingress, fire, modifications.
Tampered serial / missing packaging
Serial removed or packaging not available when required.
