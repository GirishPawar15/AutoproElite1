Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoPro Elite collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

Last updated: 2025

Information we collect

  • Account details: name, email, login information.
  • Usage data: pages visited, actions taken, basic device/browser info.
  • Listings & uploads: images and details you submit for selling cars or damage detection.
  • Support messages: when you contact us, we store the message to respond.

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve features like listings, cart, orders, and damage detection.
  • To communicate about updates, support queries, and account activity.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) only to operate the service, or when required by law.

Data retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for providing services and meeting legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the Contact page.
